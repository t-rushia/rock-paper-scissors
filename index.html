Rock | Paper | Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple hand game played between two people in this case you will be playing against the computer. The game has three possible outcomes: rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. Each time someone wins their score will be increased by 1 until either the user or computer reaches 5 points total.
